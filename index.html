Research Interests

Talking Face & Metahuman Technology

My research in this area focuses on developing realistic talking face synthesis using deep learning and computer vision techniques. I work on creating metahumans with natural facial expressions, lip synchronization, and emotional responses.

  • Audio-driven facial animation
  • Emotion-aware talking face generation
  • Cross-identity facial reenactment
  • Real-time metahuman rendering
  • Identity preservation in facial animation

Indoor Navigation

My work in indoor navigation systems addresses the challenges of precise localization and wayfinding in complex indoor environments where GPS signals are unavailable or unreliable.

  • Vision-based indoor positioning
  • Map generation and spatial understanding
  • User-centric navigation interface design
  • Machine learning for path optimization
  • Multi-sensor fusion for robust localization
